Marco Antonio Solis

Millions of hearts were captured with his songs...and he's still capturing them to this day!

Here is a timeline of Marco Antonio Solis' life:

  • 1959 - Born in Ario de Rosales, Michoacan, Mexico
  • 1965 - Began musical career at the age of six, performing part of Los Hermanitos Solis.
  • 1975 - Co-founded Los Bukis, of which he was the lead vocalist and guitarist.
  • 1996 - Solis released his first solo album after nearly two decades of succes, the band split up, with Solis pursuing a solo career,
  • 2010 - Solis received his star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
  • 2013 - Solo album "Gracias Por Estar Aqui" was released, and debuted at #1 on the Billboard's Top Latin Albums chart.
  • 2017 - Solis provided the Spanish-dubbing of Ernesto de la Cruz, the main antagonist of the Disney-Pixar film Coco.
